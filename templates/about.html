About

Audio Transcriber is a small utility for turning spoken audio into a speaker-labeled transcript.

This tool was made for those moments where you have a meeting recording, an interview, or a voice memo, and need it as readable, timestamped text instead of scrubbing through audio by hand.

Uploaded audio is transcribed and diarized locally, using OpenAI Whisper and WhisperX right on the machine running the server. No audio is ever sent to a third-party service.

This is an open source project, check out the source code.
